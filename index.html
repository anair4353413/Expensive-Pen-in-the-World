Expensive Pen in the World

Aurora Diamante

About the Pen

The Aurora Diamante is considered the most expensive pen in the world, with a price tag reaching up to $1.47 million. Crafted from solid platinum and adorned with over 30 carats of De Beers diamonds.

Unique Features

  • Solid platinum body
  • Over 30 carats of diamonds
  • Customizable engravings
  • Exclusive, limited edition
  • Handcrafted in Italy
